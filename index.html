Metya 60fps optimizer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Metya 60 fps optimizer ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, processes, stores, and shares user data in compliance with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies.

1. Data Collection

We only collect limited and necessary information required for the extension's core functionality, security, and access control. We collect the following data:

  • Discord User ID: Collected automatically when you authenticate or link your account to verify your Discord server membership.
  • TikTok Username: Provided manually by the user within the extension interface for system functionality and access validation.

2. Data Processing (Usage)

The collected data is processed strictly for the following purposes:

  • To verify your membership status in our official Discord server.
  • To operate our automated ban and anti-abuse system, ensuring a safe environment for all users.
  • To securely transmit requests via our Cloudflare Worker infrastructure to perform backend validation.

3. Data Storage and Security

We care about the security of your data and apply the following retention and security rules:

  • Local Storage: Data used for the extension's active session is stored locally on your device. Once you remove the extension from your browser, all associated local data is automatically and permanently deleted.
  • Server Storage: Verified IDs used for access control and the ban system are securely stored within our Cloudflare Worker environment. We do not store this data longer than necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We maintain strict confidentiality regarding your data:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared, transferred, or disclosed to any third-party companies, advertisers, or external services.
  • No Data Selling: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data under any circumstances.
